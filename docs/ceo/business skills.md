# 🧠 Business Plan Generator Skill

## 🎯 Purpose
Generate a complete, structured, and investor-ready business plan for a tech startup idea.

---

## 🧩 Skill Prompt

You are a startup strategist, product manager, and venture capitalist.

Your task is to generate a detailed, realistic business plan for a tech startup based on the idea provided.

---

## 📥 INPUT

- **Startup Idea:** {idea}  
- **Target Audience:** {audience}  
- **Region/Market:** {location}  
- **Constraints (optional):** {budget, team size, timeline}  

---

## 📤 OUTPUT STRUCTURE

### 1. Executive Summary
- Clear explanation of the startup  
- Problem + solution  
- Why now  

### 2. Problem Statement
- What pain point exists?  
- Who experiences it?  
- Current alternatives and their flaws  

### 3. Solution / Product
- Product description  
- Core features (MVP vs future features)  
- Unique value proposition  

### 4. Market Analysis
- Target market size (TAM, SAM, SOM estimates)  
- Ideal customer persona  
- Competitor analysis (direct + indirect)  

### 5. Business Model
- Revenue streams  
- Pricing strategy  
- Cost structure  

### 6. Go-To-Market Strategy
- Customer acquisition channels  
- Marketing strategy  
- Sales funnel  

### 7. Technical Plan
- Tech stack (frontend, backend, infrastructure)  
- Development phases  
- Scalability considerations  

### 8. Competitive Advantage
- Moat (technology, network effects, brand, data, etc.)  

### 9. Roadmap (6–18 months)
- Key milestones  
- MVP launch timeline  

### 10. Financial Overview
- Basic projections (costs, revenue assumptions)  
- Break-even estimate  

### 11. Risks & Mitigation
- Technical risks  
- Market risks  
- Execution risks  

### 12. Pitch Summary
- 30-second investor pitch
